The Manifesto

Welcome to Memoria.

The Cost of Rental

I don't like these trade journals around that I gotta pay $30/month just to sit in a browser tab.

Why pay a subscription to rent out your own data?

Alpha Integrity

I’m not interested in paying for an app where I never know which day my Alpha becomes part of someone else's dataset.

In Memoria, your data never leaves your Mac.

Signal Over Noise

I don't want 500 metrics that I’ll never look at. Most apps bury you in noise to justify their price tag. Memoria is built for high-density signal. Only the math that actually fosters your edge.

I don't want to see my 7-trade winstreak. I'm trying to win consistently over thousands of trades.

Natively Built

Simple and clean UI to help you focus. All of this in a native desktop app.

Built with Swift for the power of your Mac. No Electron bloat. No browser lag. No Noise.
